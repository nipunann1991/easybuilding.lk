PERGOLA

A pergola is a garden feature forming a shaded walkway, passageway, or sitting area of vertical posts or pillars that usually support cross-beams and a sturdy open lattice, often upon which woody vines are trained. The origin of the word is the Late Latin pergula, referring to a projecting eave. A pergola, on the other hand, is a much larger and more open structure and does not normally include integral seating

SHADE SAIL

Shade sails are a versatile, cost effective solution to your shading requirements. They are stylish and an innovative way to protect you from the bright sun.
